About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Vladimir V. Berdon, an HR professional with a diverse background in music, streaming, graphic design, and a strong passion for web development and IT.

Throughout my career, I have thrived in the dynamic field of Human Resources, where I have honed my skills in building effective teams, fostering positive work environments, and supporting employee growth. My experience has provided me with exceptional interpersonal, organizational, and problem-solving abilities that I apply to every project I undertake.

Prior to my HR journey, I had a fulfilling career as a musician, specializing in teaching cello and various other instruments. Sharing my passion for music and nurturing the talents of aspiring musicians brought me immense joy. This experience instilled in me patience, adaptability, and the ability to creatively engage with students of different learning styles. It also reinforced my belief in the power of art to inspire, uplift, and connect people.

As my interests evolved, I delved into the world of streaming, where I found an exciting platform to engage with a broader audience. Alongside my streaming endeavors, I dabbled in graphic design, creating visually captivating content to enhance my online presence. This experience allowed me to develop an eye for aesthetics, an understanding of user experience, and a desire to explore the intersection of design and technology.

Motivated by my passion for the digital realm, I am currently immersed in studying web development and sharpening my skills in IT. I am fascinated by the endless possibilities of coding languages and their ability to create dynamic, user-friendly websites. This journey allows me to combine my creative and analytical skills, fostering a harmonious balance between aesthetics and functionality.

I am deeply committed to continuous growth and embrace challenges that broaden my horizons. Thank you for taking the time to explore my portfolio. If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaborations, please feel free to reach out. I look forward to connecting with you!
